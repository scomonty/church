Sonshine and Rainbows Discovery Center

We enroll children 2 weeks to 6 years old. Preschool is also available for ages 2 1/2 to school age during the school year. Hours are 7:30 am to 5:30 pm Monday through Friday.

Our Mission

Sonshine and Rainbows Discovery Center is dedicated to providing an atmosphere conducive to physical, emotional, and educational development based on Christian values for children two weeks to six years old through day care and preschool units.
