Welcome to 1st United Methodist Church of Greensburg, Kansas.
This church has been serving the Greensburg community for over 125 year.

On May 4, 2007, our church building was completely destroyed by an EF5 tornado. As a congregation we have been through several transitions after the storm. We have gone from a tent to a trailer, to the first phase of our building process. We moved into the new sanctuary on Easter Sunday, 2010. Throughout this process the congregation has grown spiritually as we have seen the Lord work in miraculous ways.
